What each node measures

Per-stall occupancy

Magnetic latch + IR pair per stall reports occupied / free / out-of-service, powering the live availability count on the dashboard and map pins.

Ammonia (NH₃) concentration

The strongest single proxy for odour and hygiene. Readings above 25 ppm automatically raise a cleaning alert for that facility.

Water tank & soap levels

Ultrasonic level sensors forecast when a refill will be needed, so consumables are dispatched before they run out — not after.

Footfall

Entry counts per facility reveal daily usage curves, which drive crew scheduling and identify under-served locations.
